# Stakeholder Mapping Template

## Purpose
This template helps identify, categorise, and manage stakeholders to ensure effective communication and engagement throughout delivery.

---

## Stakeholder Identification

| Stakeholder | Role | Influence | Interest |
|------------|------|-----------|----------|
| Business Sponsor | Executive | High | High |
| Product Owner | Delivery | High | High |
| End Users | Operational | Medium | High |
| IT Team | Technical | High | Medium |

---

## Stakeholder Classification
- **High Influence / High Interest**: Manage closely
- **High Influence / Low Interest**: Keep satisfied
- **Low Influence / High Interest**: Keep informed
- **Low Influence / Low Interest**: Monitor

---

## Communication Plan

| Stakeholder Group | Communication Method | Frequency |
|------------------|----------------------|-----------|
| Executives | Status report | Monthly |
| Delivery Team | Stand-ups | Daily |
| End Users | Workshops | Bi-weekly |

---

## Key Risks
- Conflicting stakeholder priorities
- Resistance to change

## Mitigation Strategies
- Early engagement and workshops
- Clear ownership and escalation paths
