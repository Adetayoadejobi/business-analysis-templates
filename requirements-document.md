# Business Requirements Document (BRD)

## Purpose
This document captures high-level business requirements to ensure alignment between business objectives and proposed solutions.

---

## 1. Background & Objectives
### Background
The organisation is undertaking a digital transformation initiative to improve operational efficiency and customer experience.

### Objectives
- Improve process efficiency
- Enable better decision-making through data visibility
- Support scalable and future-ready solutions

---

## 2. Scope
### In Scope
- Business process analysis
- System requirements definition
- Data and reporting needs

### Out of Scope
- Technical design decisions

## 4. Functional Requirements
- FR1: The system shall allow users to view consolidated customer data.
- FR2: The system shall support role-based access control.

---

## 5. Non-Functional Requirements
- Performance: Pages must load within 3 seconds.
- Security: Data access must comply with GDPR.
- Availability: System uptime of 99.9%.

- Integration with existing CRM systems
- Fixed delivery timelines
